Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Delicious thick sticky glaze on moist chicken. Can be served with rice pilaf with broccoli bits & slivered carrots & sliced almonds, & green salads as side dishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil togheter in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
